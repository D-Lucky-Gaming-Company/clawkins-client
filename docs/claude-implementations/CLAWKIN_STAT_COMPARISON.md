## Role Comparison

### Swee'pea - Tanky Bruiser
**Strengths:**
- Highest HP pool (230 at max)
- Highest DEF (175 at max)
- Excellent sustain with Stretch & Nap
- Can outlast most opponents

**Weaknesses:**
- Lowest SPEED (57 at max)
- Acts last in most battles
- Lower offensive pressure

**Playstyle:**
- Tank damage and wear down opponents
- Use healing to maintain HP
- Rely on defense to reduce incoming damage

### Ginger - Fast Attacker
**Strengths:**
- Highest SPEED (160 at max)
- Highest ATK (170 at max, tied with Dart)
- Strikes first almost always
- Devastating offensive pressure

**Weaknesses:**
- Lowest HP pool (110 at max)
- Low DEF (75 at max)
- Very fragile - can't take many hits

**Playstyle:**
- Strike first and strike hard
- Overwhelm enemies before they can respond
- High risk, high reward
- Requires careful HP management

### Dart - Speed Attacker
**Strengths:**
- High SPEED (140 at max)
- High ATK (170 at max, tied with Ginger)
- Good balance of speed and durability
- More HP than Ginger

**Weaknesses:**
- Lower SPEED than Ginger
- Still fragile with low DEF
- Middle ground between Swee'pea and Ginger

**Playstyle:**
- Fast attacker with slightly more survivability
- Strike quickly but can take a few hits
- Balanced aggressive approach

## Battle Dynamics

### Turn Order (Level 30)
1. **Ginger** (SPEED 160) - Almost always first
2. **Dart** (SPEED 140) - Usually second
3. **Swee'pea** (SPEED 57) - Almost always last

### Damage Output (Level 30)
1. **Ginger/Dart** (ATK 170) - Highest damage
2. **Swee'pea** (ATK 110) - Moderate damage

### Survivability (Level 30)
1. **Swee'pea** (HP 230, DEF 175) - Extremely tanky
2. **Dart** (HP 120, DEF 75) - Moderate durability
3. **Ginger** (HP 110, DEF 75) - Glass cannon

## Team Composition Strategies

### Balanced Team
- **Swee'pea** (tank) + **Ginger** (fast attacker) + **Dart** (speed attacker)
- Covers all roles: tank, speed, and offense

### Offensive Team
- **Ginger** + **Dart** + **Dart**
- Maximum speed and damage
- High risk strategy

### Defensive Team
- **Swee'pea** + **Swee'pea** + **Ginger**
- Two tanks with one fast finisher
- Wear down opponents

## Recommended Starter

### For New Players: **Swee'pea**
- Most forgiving
- High survivability
- Easier to learn with

### For Experienced Players: **Ginger**
- High skill ceiling
- Requires careful play
- Maximum offensive potential

### For Balanced Play: **Dart**
- Middle ground
- Good speed and damage
- Moderate survivability

## Matchup Analysis

### Swee'pea vs Ginger
- Swee'pea tanks Ginger's attacks
- Ginger struggles to break through DEF
- Swee'pea wins through sustain

### Swee'pea vs Dart
- Similar to Ginger matchup
- Dart has slightly more HP
- Swee'pea still wins through defense

### Ginger vs Dart
- Ginger strikes first (SPEED advantage)
- Both have similar ATK and DEF
- Ginger wins through speed advantage

## Summary

- **Swee'pea**: Best tank, highest survivability, slowest
- **Ginger**: Best speed, highest damage potential, most fragile
- **Dart**: Balanced speed attacker, middle ground

Each Clawkin has a distinct identity and playstyle, encouraging strategic team building and tactical decisions.
